Raw Data

Just a little bit of information about certain columns that are more ambiguous than others. The sibsp column is the number of siblings/spouses also aboard the titanic. The parch column is the number of parents/children also aboard the titanic. Embarked is the port of embarkation: C = Cherbourg, Q = Queenstown, S = Southampton. The ages that are listed as xx.5 are estimates.
